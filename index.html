Design thesis

Professional research sites should be both newspaper and notebook.

TuringWire-style clarity gives the reader an immediate reason to care. Gwern-style durability gives the work a shelf life: citations, metadata, long-form argument, and an archive that can be revisited years later.

Operating principles

The article is the product.
Navigation supports the essay; it does not compete with it.
Metadata is interpretation.
Date, source, category, and confidence tell readers how to use a note.
Archive beats feed.
Search, tags, and chronology make research cumulative rather than disposable.
